  • Mathematics Background
  • English Proficiency
Personal Documents

Important Information

Registration Deadlines

Applications for the upcoming academic year must be submitted by March 31st, 2025. Late applications may be considered based on availability.

Application Fees

Application fee: 50,000 MMK (non-refundable). Payment can be made through bank transfer or in person at the MIIT office.

Document Submission

All required documents must be submitted in original or certified copies. Digital copies are accepted for initial review.

Interview Process

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview. The interview will assess academic readiness and motivation for the program.

Strategic Partnership

Mentoring Myanmar Institute of Information Technology:

On 28th May 2012, the Government of the Republic of the Union of Myanmar and the Government of India signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to establish MIIT at Mandalay. IIIT-B has been chosen to act as the mentor institution, ensuring curriculum development, faculty training, research collaboration, and student exchange programs.

The collaboration includes joint workshops, conferences, and seminars to encourage innovation and research. Students and faculty from MIIT get opportunities to work on real-world projects under the guidance of IIIT-B experts.

This partnership strengthens bilateral ties between Myanmar and India in the field of Information Technology and serves as a model for future academic collaborations in the region.

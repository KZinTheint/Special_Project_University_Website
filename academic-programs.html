Bachelor of Computer Science and Engineering

5 Years

A comprehensive program covering computer science fundamentals, software development, algorithms, and data structures. Students learn programming languages, database systems, and software engineering principles.

  • Software Development
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Database Management
  • Web Development
  • Mobile App Development
  • Artificial Intelligence

Bachelor of Electronic and Communication Engineering

5 Years

Focuses on electronic systems, communication technologies, signal processing, and telecommunications. Students gain expertise in designing and implementing electronic communication systems.

  • Electronic Circuit Design
  • Communication Systems

  • Wireless Communication

Master of Engineering (Computer Science)

2 Years

An advanced program designed for graduates seeking to deepen their knowledge in computer science and specialize in cutting-edge technologies. The program combines rigorous coursework with research opportunities.

Year 1: Course Work
  • Advanced Algorithms & Data Structures
  • Machine Learning & AI
  • Advanced Software Engineering
  • Research Methodology
  • Elective Specializations
Year 2: Thesis
  • Research Project Design
  • Literature Review
  • Data Collection & Analysis
  • Thesis Writing & Defense
  • Publication Opportunities
Learn More

Curriculum Structure

Foundation Courses (Years 1-2)

Mathematics, Physics, Programming Fundamentals, Computer Architecture, Data Structures, and Basic Engineering Principles.

Core Courses (Years 2-4)

Advanced Programming, Database Systems, Operating Systems, Computer Networks, Software Engineering, and Specialized Electives.

Project Work (Year 4-5)

Capstone Projects, Industry Internships, Research Projects, and Final Year Thesis focusing on real-world applications.

Professional Development

Communication Skills, Entrepreneurship, Ethics in Computing, Industry Certifications, and Career Preparation.

MIIT Academic Calendar

Standard Academic Structure

The standard academic calendar consists of two semesters. The first semester of the academic year normally starts in December and ends in March. The second semester starts in June and finishes in October.

Semester Structure

Chronological Order Semester 1 Semester 2 Remarks
Lecture Period 15 weeks 15 weeks The lecture starts from Monday to Friday.
Private Study 1 week 1 week One-week private study period is available for all students.
Examination 2 weeks 2 weeks Students have to take their exam after the private study period.
Total Duration 18 weeks 18 weeks
Vacation 4-5 weeks 8-9 weeks The duration of the first semester is from December to March and the second semester is from June to October

MIIT History & International Collaborations

Foundation Year

MIIT was established in 2015 as a pioneering institution dedicated to Information Technology education in Myanmar, marking the beginning of a new era in technological education for the country.

IIIT-B Collaboration

MIIT has established a strong partnership with the International Institute of Information Technology Bangalore (IIIT-B), India, fostering academic exchange, research collaboration, and curriculum development.

India-Myanmar Collaboration

Through strategic partnerships with Indian educational institutions and technology companies, MIIT has strengthened its position as a bridge between Myanmar and India's thriving IT sector, creating opportunities for student exchange programs, joint research initiatives, and industry connections.

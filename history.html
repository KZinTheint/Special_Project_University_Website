Our History

A Journey of Excellence in Information Technology Education

University History

The Myanmar Institute of Information Technology (MIIT) was established as a National Centre of Excellence in 2015, following a Memorandum of Understanding between the Government of the Republic of the Union of Myanmar and the Government of the Republic of India. Since its inception, MIIT has been dedicated to providing high-quality education and research in the field of Information Technology.

Timeline

  • 2015 MIIT Established

    MIIT is founded as a result of collaboration between Myanmar and India, aiming to become a leading institution in IT education.

  • 2016 First Academic Year

    The first batch of students is admitted, and undergraduate programs in Computer Science and Engineering, and Electronics and Communications Engineering are launched.

  • 2018 First Graduation Ceremony

    MIIT celebrates its first cohort of graduates, marking a significant milestone in its journey.

  • 2020 Expansion of Programs

    New academic and research programs are introduced, further strengthening MIIT's role in the IT sector.

  • 2023 International Collaborations

    MIIT establishes partnerships with international universities and organizations to enhance research and student opportunities.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a leading institution in Information Technology education and research, contributing to the development of Myanmar and the global community.

Mission: To provide quality education, foster innovation, and promote research in Information Technology and related fields.

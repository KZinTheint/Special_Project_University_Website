Admission Requirements & Fees

Everything you need to know about applying to MIIT and the associated costs

Important Notice

Eligibility: Only students who passed the university entrance exam in 2023 are eligible to apply. Previous year students are not accepted.

Official Admission Requirements

Below is the official document outlining all admission requirements for MIIT:

Official MIIT Admission Requirements Document

Admission Requirements

All applicants must submit the following documents:

1

Mark Sheet: Original or copy of your academic mark sheet

2

Certificates/Recommendations: Original or copy of certificates and recommendation letters

3

ID Cards: Copy of guardian's and applicant's ID cards

4

Household List: Copy of household registration list

5

Police Recommendation: Original police recommendation letter

6

Ward Recommendation: Original ward recommendation letter

7

Passport Photos: 5 recent passport-sized photographs

Examination Details

Subjects

The entrance examination covers:

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
Examination Format

The test will be conducted using a Computer Based System (CBS) to ensure:

  • Efficient evaluation process
  • Accurate results
  • Modern testing methodology

Admission Fees & Costs

69,000 MMK
Annual Fee (Per Year)

Complete annual fee covering all academic costs

25,000 MMK
Hostel Fee (Per Semester)

Accommodation fee for on-campus housing per semester

Total First Year Cost

119,000 MMK

Includes annual fee (69,000 MMK) + hostel fees for 2 semesters (50,000 MMK)

* Fees are subject to change. Please contact admissions office for current rates.
